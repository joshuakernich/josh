I'm Josh, and I love digital education.

Combining my unique skill-set of Learning Design, UX, storytelling, and front-end programming, I've created my own approach to innovative digital learning. Throughout my 13+ years in the e-learning industry, I have designed hundreds of small and large learning projects in topics from mathematics to mental health, parenting, and stroke therapy.

In recent years, I have been working remotely, supporting a range of organisations in producing rigorous digital products and educational materials all over the world.

My goal is to influence a positive future trajectory in digital education through development of world-leading products, frameworks, and mentorship of the next generation.

Noteable Projects

Family Man

Movember Foundation (2019-2021)

  • Learning Experience Design
  • Learning Design
  • Script Writing

Family Man involved close collaboration with Mark Dadds, a world-leading expert in parenting techniques. My role was to glean his behavioural intervention model, and design an elegant learning experience to help parents navigate problem behaviour.

Through mapping of learning outcomes, and scripting matching scenarios, my team and I created a interactive, branching web application to help users experiment and learn strong parenting techniques.

This product is live at https://familyman.movember.com

Conversations

Movember Foundation (2019-2021)

  • Learning Experience Design
  • UX Design
  • UI Design
  • Script Writing

Given an open brief, I led a cohort of designers in designing a product to help men have more productive conversations around their mental health.

Utilising existing conversation models and integrating them into a series of scripted practice conversations allowed learners to investigate and learn in a way that closely approximates the challenges of having tough conversations.

This product is live at https://conversations.movember.com

Gripable Games

Gripable (2017 - 2022)

  • UX Design
  • Game Design
  • Management of Development Team

With Gripable, I led the brainstorming, design, and development of games designed to support the therapeutic outcomes of patients who have diminished grip strength or hand articulation.

This required carefully balancing the needs of Neuroscientists, Occupational Therapists, the capabilities of the development team, and the desire to make the games fun.

Product details are available at https://gripable.co
